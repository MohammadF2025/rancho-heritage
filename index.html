Health Benefits of Olive Oil

Extra virgin olive oil is rich in monounsaturated fats and antioxidants. Research suggests it can help:

  • Support heart health by improving cholesterol levels
  • Reduce inflammation thanks to polyphenols
  • Support brain health and cognitive function
  • Provide antioxidants that protect cells from oxidative stress

Note: This information is educational and not medical advice. Consult a healthcare provider for personalized guidance.
